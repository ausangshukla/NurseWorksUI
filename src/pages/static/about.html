About

NurseWorks is a scalable digital platform with a strong commitment to hospital based nursing of high clinical quality and convenience. We are strongly commitment to the quality and dignity of the Nursing profession.

We review professional experiences and credential only the best in the profession to support them with full time, part-time and international opportunities. We believe that doing this will help each nurse undertake her individual journey to learn, earn, grow at her own pace balancing career vs. personal commitments, and help us develop a scalable digital platform that engages nurses meaningfully over the lifetime of their career.

We empanel quality hospitals to use our platform to support their full time and part time nursing needs in a high quality and efficient manner through digital technology. By blending digital technology with outstanding customer service, we can attract, retain and serve your hospital with the highest standards of support for quality nurses.
